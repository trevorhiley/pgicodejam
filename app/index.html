Company Bio

Lending Ledger was created by a group of real estate technology professionals who had a vision of making the mortgage lending process seamless for both the borrower and lender. By utilizing blockchain technology we were able to create a streamline application process that provides benefits for both the borrowers and the lenders by eliminating the need for brokers to facilitate the transaction.
